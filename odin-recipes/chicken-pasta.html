Chicken Pasta

Description

This Chicken Pasta recipe combines tender pieces of chicken with a creamy sauce and al dente pasta. It’s perfect for a hearty lunch or dinner and offers a blend of Italian flavors with a twist.

The dish is quick to prepare and can be easily customized with your favorite herbs and vegetables. Enjoy a restaurant-quality meal at home!

Ingredients

For the Pasta

  • 400g of your favorite pasta
  • Salt for boiling water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Chicken Sauce

  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 100ml chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken cubes and season with salt and pepper. Cook until golden and cooked through, then remove from the pan.
  3. Add a little more oil to the pan if needed, then sauté the chopped onion until translucent. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  4. Return the chicken to the pan. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, then add the basil and oregano. Bring to a simmer and let the sauce thicken slightly.
  5. Mix in the cooked pasta and toss until well combined. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
