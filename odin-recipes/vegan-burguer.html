Vegan Burger

Description

This Vegan Burger is a hearty and delicious plant-based alternative to the traditional burger. Packed with flavor and nutrients, it's perfect for a healthy yet satisfying meal.

The burger features a well-seasoned patty served on a toasted bun with fresh lettuce, tomato, and your favorite vegan condiments. Enjoy a guilt-free treat!

Ingredients

For the Patty

  • 1 can (400g) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Assembly

  • 4 whole grain vegan burger buns
  • Fresh lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomato
  • Sliced red onion
  • Your favorite vegan sauce or mustard

Steps

  1. Mash the black beans in a bowl until mostly smooth but still a bit chunky.
  2. Add the rolled oats, chopped onion, garlic, tomato paste, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are combined. If the mixture is too dry, add a splash of water.
  3. Form the mixture into 4 even patties.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and cook the patties for about 5-6 minutes on each side until they are firm and lightly browned.
  5. Toast the burger buns lightly on the pan, then assemble your burgers with the patties, lettuce, tomato, red onion, and your favorite vegan sauce.
