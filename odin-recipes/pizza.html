Homemade Pizza

Description

This is a delicious homemade pizza with a crispy crust and fresh toppings. Made with carefully selected ingredients and lots of love, it's perfect for a family dinner or a casual get-together.

Enjoy the authentic taste of pizza made from scratch, and feel free to experiment with different toppings!

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 1/2 kg wheat flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 sachet of active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 cups warm water

For the Toppings

  • Tomato sauce
  • 3 tomatoes, sliced
  • 300g mozzarella cheese, cut into chunks (not pre-sliced)
  • Oregano to taste

Steps

  1. Mix all the dough ingredients and knead until smooth. Allow the dough to rise until it doubles in size.
  2. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface and spread the tomato sauce evenly over it.
  3. Add the tomato slices and mozzarella cheese, then sprinkle oregano on top.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C (392°F) for about 15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
